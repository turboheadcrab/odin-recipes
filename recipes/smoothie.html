Coconut Berry Smoothie Recipe

Description

Beyond the perfect balance of flavors, there are also a lot of health benefits to appreciate in this recipe as well. Bananas, although high in carbohydrates and natural sugars, are also the perfect way to naturally boost your energy first thing in the morning. Bananas are also known for their balance of potassium and vitamin C, which is something all of our bodies need more than ever. The berries in this smoothie are lower in sugar and are packed with antioxidants. They compliment the chia seeds in this recipe as well to give your digestive system a rush of fiber to aid in overall weight loss.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz container unsweetened coconut yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 medium banana, sliced and frozen
  • 1/2 cup non-dairy milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed meal
  • ice as needed to thicken

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender, blend until smooth.
